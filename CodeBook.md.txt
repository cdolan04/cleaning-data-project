* `activity` - The activity performed

#### Activity Labels
Provided as part of the Raw/Source Data. The Following activities were performed as measurements were taken:

* `WALKING` (`1`): walking
* `WALKING_UPSTAIRS` (`2`): walking up a stairs
* `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS` (`3`): walking down a stairs
* `SITTING` (`4`): sitting
* `STANDING` (`5`): standing
* `LAYING` (`6`): laying down
